# Manuscript files

The manuscript text, figures, and tables are not included in this repository due to publisher license restrictions.

To reproduce, download the paper and supplementary materials from the following DOI and place them in this directory:

- **DOI**: https://doi.org/10.1038/s41586-022-04518-2
- **Citation**: Emont, M.P., Jacobs, C., Essene, A.L. et al. A single-cell atlas of human and mouse white adipose tissue. Nature 603, 926-933 (2022).

Expected files:
- `manuscript.txt` — plain text of the manuscript
- `manuscript.html` — HTML of the manuscript
- `figures/` — manuscript figures
- `tables/` — supplementary tables
